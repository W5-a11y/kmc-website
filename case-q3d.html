Built for the Real World — Q3D Sensing

AuraGo by Q3D Sensing is a portable LiDAR scanner that brings professional-grade 3D capture to smartphones and tablets, making precise spatial scanning accessible to everyday workflows. The film follows the device into the field, showing how a small piece of hardware quietly reshapes how teams measure, document, and design the world around them.

Produced by KMC+LUCAS, the campaign balances technical clarity with cinematic restraint — a brand film grounded in real use, not staged demos.
