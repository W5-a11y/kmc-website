What makes a house a home? — Aloe Blacc

In collaboration with Aloe Blacc, this documentary-style film explores the meaning of home in the aftermath of devastating wildfires that reshaped his hometown. Through intimate reflection, the story moves beyond the idea of a physical house, revealing home as a place of identity, memory, and return—a space that carries generational strength and extends safety through community.

Produced by KMC+LUCAS, the film transforms real experience into a quiet yet powerful narrative about resilience, belonging, and the deeper essence of home.
