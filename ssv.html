Surviving Silicon Valley is the first reality series in the United States to use real estate as its central narrative, blending human exploration with real individuals telling their own authentic life stories.

Each episode uses a home as the medium to reveal the emotional crossroads and turning points in a person's life—uncovering the tension between human nature, money, family, and self-identity.

Surviving Silicon Valley
Premiere & Screening

Under the lights of Plug and Play, Surviving Silicon Valley made its debut—surrounded by a crowd of people all navigating their own versions of life in Silicon Valley. It wasn't just a screening; it felt like a shared moment of recognition.

Positioned as the Valley's first real estate reality series, the show doesn't hold back. It puts everything on the table—personal truths, life choices, family dynamics, identity, and the quiet struggles behind ambition.

The premiere may be over—but the story is only just beginning.
